Welcome to the West Coast Rave Community website. This site is meant to be an extension to our facebook group "West Coast Rave Community - WCRC." The group is a place to help facillitate connecting EVERY raver on the west coast and provide a platform to share music and videos, talk about anything and everything rave related including shows, meeting new people, having a great times, and letting people know where shows and what shows people are going to.

Use this website to help spread the word to any and all ravers across the West Coast, lets start a movement! As always this is meant to be a place of positive vibes, ever vigilant in our quest to
Save one Raver at a Time.
